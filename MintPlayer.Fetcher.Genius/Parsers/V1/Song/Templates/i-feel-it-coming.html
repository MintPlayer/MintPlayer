About “I Feel It Coming”

Inspired by house-and ‘80s pop, “I Feel It Coming” is about wanting more from a relationship. The song differs from The Weeknd’s usual attitudes towards love, which revolve around one-night stands and lighthearted fun.

With a catchy but laid-back chorus, a danceable sound, and Abel’s impressive vocal performance, “I Feel It Coming” is yet another Weeknd track that draws a comparison to Michael Jackson.

The song was one of two singles released by The Weeknd on November 17, 2016, alongside “Party Monster.”

  • Have Daft Punk and The Weeknd collaborated before?

    On Starboy’s opening track and lead single, Daft Punk assisted The Weeknd with their vocals and production skills on “Starboy.”

  • Was this track influenced by Michael Jackson?

    Yes, there is definitely a Michael Jackson-vibe featured throughout The Weeknd’s vocal performance. MJ has always been one of Abel’s biggest influences:

    Michael, man, that guy was the star. He invented the star. There will never be another Michael. I want to make it very clear that I’m not trying to be Michael. He’s everything to me, so you’re going to hear it in my music. Off the Wall was the album that inspired me to sing. It helped me find my [voice]. I didn’t understand what songwriting was before Off the Wall. ‘Don’t Stop Till You Get Enough,’ I kept singing that, and I found my falsetto.

    It wasn’t until Bad when I wanted to be a songwriter. ‘Dirty Diana’ is my favorite song of all time, it is literally my No. 1. It takes all these genres and it puts it into one song – rock, experimental, hip-hop, R&B. It’s why I wanted to write these dope songs.

  • What are some notable songs Doc McKinney has produced?

    Doc McKinney is one of The Weeknd’s go-to producers. He has worked with Abel since the Trilogy era. Some notable songs of his include:

    • “Starboy”
    • “Wicked Games”
    • “The Zone”
    • “Drunk In Love (Remix)”

  • What notable songs has Cirkut worked on?

    Cirkut is a bonafide hit maker, and has worked with artists like Nicki Minaj, Miley Cyrus and Katy Perry. Notable songs of his include:

    • The Weeknd’s “Starboy”
    • Nicki Minaj’s “Only”
    • Miley Cyrus' “Wrecking Ball”
    • R.City’s “Locked Away”
    • Katy Perry’s “Dark Horse”

  • What notable songs has Daft Punk contributed production to?

    Other than their successful solo discography, Daft Punk has been an influential duo behind the boards. Some notable songs include:

    • Kanye West’s “Black Skinhead”
    • The Weeknd’s “Starboy”
    • N.E.R.D.’s “Hypnotize U”

  • What did The Weeknd say about collaborating with Daft Punk?

    During his Zane Lowe Beats 1 interview:

    These guys are like my idols, and idols to, you know, most artists. Their branding… It’s unbelievable. I was definitely inspired by that, at the beginning of my career, cause nobody knew how I looked as well… So being able to work with them felt like a bucket list, so I really wanted to fight for that. And we got into the studio, and you know what it is man, you get into the studio with artists and it’s either it’s gonna be amazing, or let’s just stay friends… And we literally did “Starboy” and “I Feel It Coming” in like two days.

  • What's the way Daft Punk modifies his voice on Chorus? Is it a talkbox?

    It’s a vocoder; an instrument that essentially makes vocals sound like instruments

  • What certifications did this track receive?

    “I Feel It Coming” was certified 4x platinum by the RIAA on March 13, 2020.
