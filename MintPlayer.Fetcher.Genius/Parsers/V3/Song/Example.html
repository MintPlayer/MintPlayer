About “Sunset Jesus”

“Sunset Jesus” is a melodic, uplifting track released on Avicii’s second studio album Stories. The track features Avicii collaborator and vocalist Sandro Cavazza for the first time. Sandro would later go on to work with Tim on tracks such as “Without You” and “Forever Yours”.

Sunset Jesus has Cavazza talking about the golden skies of sunny California and his dreams for his lifetime.
